<h4>Nevada <i>(Winter 2024)</i></h4>
<p> We explored a variety of historic (and modern, thanks to Angelo Gold Ashanti) gold mines in and around Beatty, Nevada on our hunt for fluorite from the American Southwest. This field trip also included some tourist side quests to Death Valley and the Hoover Dam! </p>
<p> <strong>Field Team:</strong> Stephen Cox, Jenna Everard, Sidney Hemming, Troy Rasbury, Bill Holt, Katie Wooton, Paul Tomascak, Steven Jaret, Sean Tvelia, Kevin Hatton, Angelo Gold Ashanti Geologists</p>
