<h4>New Mexico <i>(Spring 2024)</i></h4>

<h4>Nevada <i>(Winter 2024)</i></h4>

<p> <strong>Field Team:</strong> Stephen Cox, Jenna Everard, Sidney Hemming, Troy Rasbury, Bill Holt, Katie Wooton, Paul Tomascak, Steven Jaret, Sean Tvelia, Kevin Hatton, Angelo Gold Ashanti Geologists</p>
